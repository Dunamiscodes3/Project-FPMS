💡 Agronomic Tips
💧

Water Management

Maintain 5cm water depth during tillering. Drain fields 2 weeks before harvest for easier mechanization.

🌱

Seed Selection

Use certified seeds from the cooperative. Basmati varieties perform best in Mwea irrigation conditions.

🧪

Soil Health

Target soil pH of 6.0–7.0 for optimal rice production. Apply lime if your pH falls below 5.5.

🚜

Machinery Timing

Book machinery at least 2 weeks before peak harvest to avoid scheduling delays and bottlenecks.
